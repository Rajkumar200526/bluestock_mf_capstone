# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Findings

## Key Insights

### 1. Mutual Fund Industry Growth

The mutual fund industry showed consistent growth from 2022 to 2025 across NAV, AUM, SIP inflows, and folio counts.
**Reference:** nav_trend.html, folio_growth.png

### 2. AUM Leadership

SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest Assets Under Management (AUM) among all fund houses, indicating strong investor confidence.
**Reference:** aum_growth.png

### 3. SIP Inflow Trend

Monthly SIP inflows increased steadily over the analysis period, reflecting growing retail participation in mutual funds.
**Reference:** sip_trend.html

### 4. Record SIP Inflow

December 2025 recorded the highest SIP inflow of ₹31,002 Crore, marking an all-time high for the industry.
**Reference:** sip_trend.html

### 5. Fund Category Dominance

Equity funds accounted for the largest share of schemes, indicating investor preference for long-term wealth creation.
**Reference:** fund_category_distribution.png

### 6. Investor Demographics

Investors in the 26–35 age group contributed the highest investment volume compared to other age groups.
**Reference:** age_distribution.png, age_boxplot.png

### 7. Geographic Participation

T30 cities generated a larger share of investments than B30 cities, although B30 participation continued to increase.
**Reference:** city_tier.png

### 8. Digital Payment Adoption

UPI emerged as the most frequently used payment method for mutual fund investments.
**Reference:** payment_mode.png

### 9. KYC Compliance

The majority of investors were KYC verified, indicating strong regulatory compliance across the investor base.
**Reference:** kyc_status.png

### 10. Fund Correlation

Funds within similar categories exhibited strong positive return correlations, suggesting sigit statusmilar market exposure and risk behavior.
**Reference:** correlation_matrix.png

## Conclusion

The EDA highlights strong growth in the Indian mutual fund industry driven by increasing SIP participation, expanding investor base, and rising AUM levels. Equity-oriented funds remain the preferred investment choice, while digital payment adoption and KYC compliance continue to strengthen the investment ecosystem.
