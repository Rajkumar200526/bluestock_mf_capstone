# Day 6 Advanced Analytics Findings

## Key Insights

1. Historical VaR and CVaR analysis identified funds with the highest downside risk during adverse market conditions.

2. Rolling 90-Day Sharpe Ratio analysis showed that top-performing funds maintained stronger risk-adjusted returns over time.

3. The 2024 investor cohort contributed the highest total investment volume and represented the largest investor segment.

4. SIP continuity analysis highlighted investors with irregular contribution patterns, allowing identification of at-risk investors.

5. Sector concentration analysis using the Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI) revealed differences in portfolio diversification across mutual fund schemes.

## Conclusion

Advanced analytics provided deeper insights into risk, investor behavior, portfolio concentration, and fund performance. The analysis supports data-driven investment evaluation and enhances the mutual fund analytics platform with risk management and recommendation capabilities.
